3eja Mergez

10Dt

Ojja is a Tunisian dish made from eggs, known for its ease of preparation. The main ingredients are eggs, tomatoes, peppers and spices (garlic, salt, pepper and caraway), all cooked in olive oil. Other ingredients can be added, possibly merguez.

S7ane Kafteji

5DT

It is a mixture of potatoes, tomatoes, green peppers, eggs, pumpkin and possibly zucchini, all coarsely chopped, fried then mixed and re-cut into small cubes. The mixture is then salted, seasoned with harissa and served with finely chopped parsley and onion. The dish is traditionally accompanied by grilled foie or merguez or even a fried egg.

S7ane Tounsi

5Dt

also called Tunisian dish or Tunisian plate, is a Tunisian egg-based dish, known for its ease of preparation. The main ingredients are eggs, harissa, méchouia salad, boiled potatoes, olives and olive oil
